Weekly Allowance and Spendings
of High School Students

This study aims to figure out the weekly allowance & expenses of high school students from grade 7-grade 12 in Davao City. 50 high school students were surveyed through the use of google forms to collect data that could benefit the study. The data collected included their grade level, weekly allowance, total spendings in school, and where they spend their remaining money/allowance. In this site, we will introduce you to the data we have collected from our study and the conclusions we have withdrawn from it.
